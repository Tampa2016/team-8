HandiHelpr

About The Christopher & Dana Reeve Foundation


The HandiHelpr app is designed to help people with disabilities find and communicate with business and companies. The idea is to connect people with different abilities to come together in order to for a community. This app acts as a site where people can rate and find places to go based on their access for disabled.


Our Values


MISSION: The Reeve Foundation is dedicated to curing spinal cord injury by funding innovative research, and improving the quality of life for people living with paralysis through grants, information and advocacy.


VISION: Our vision is to create one large community of people who are dedicated and determined to keeo companies and business aware of a lot of problems that disabled people.
